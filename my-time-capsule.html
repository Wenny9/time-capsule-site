Design Concept

My time capsule is an abstract “bear silhouette” built from layered, translucent shells. The form feels soft and protective, like memory itself — gentle, diffused, and never perfectly sharp. The central cavity is left intentionally spacious, so that small objects can be placed inside like a warm “glow” of time.

Materials are chosen for translucency and lightness (PP sheets or frosted acetate). Each layer follows a simplified contour to create depth without heaviness. When illuminated from within, the capsule turns memories into a quiet, warm halo.
