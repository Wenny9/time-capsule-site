My Time Capsule — Design Concept

I designed the capsule as an abstract bear silhouette built from five translucent layers. Each layer is a slice of memory. When warm light diffuses through the hollow core, emotion becomes legible again.

The primary material is semi-transparent PP sheets (or translucent 3D-printed resin), finished in a soft pink-gold glow to symbolize warmth preserved across time. In this capsule, memory is treated as light — it diffuses, overlaps, and breathes.
